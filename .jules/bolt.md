## 2024-05-18 - Optimized receipt syncing with concurrent Promise.all chunking
**Learning:** Awaiting async file processing in sequential loops (like downloading and saving localized receipts) drastically blocks network throughput and extends the process time linearly (O(n)). But fully parallel unbounded concurrency crashes network requests on scaling to larger sets of items.
**Action:** Transformed iterative loops in `syncAllReceipts` into concurrent request pools mapped into arrays of task closures, evaluated with `Promise.all` in fixed batches of 5. Execution of 10 expense downloads for three books (30 receipts overall + simulated delay of 10ms per receipt) took 312ms sequentially versus 64ms batched parallel.
